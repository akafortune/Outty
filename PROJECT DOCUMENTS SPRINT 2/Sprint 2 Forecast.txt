Sprint #2 Forecast

	Our forecast for this sprint is that we will be able to finish around 60-70 story points inside of this next sprint, 
	this largely outscales our last sprint where we finished around 30 story points. This would seem to violate the Yesterday's Weather 
	method, where if we assume that team size and sprint length remain consistent then the achievable number of story points should be the same. 
	I feel that this would not be a proper heuristic to evaluate this sprint with, when we began the last sprint none of us knew any Flutter / Dart 
	(much less any adjacent language like React). We now know this language and are able to operate more efficiently. You could argue that we have 
	a similar challenge this sprint with learning Firebase, however our preliminary research indicates that Firebase is a lot easier to use in a simple 
	fashion than Dart/Flutter is. We propose that we are around twice as efficient as we were in sprint 1, and that learning firebase will not significantly 
	slow us down, so we should be able to accomplish around double the amount of story points (60-70 points).
